About me

I was born on June 4th, 2001 in Miami, FL. I've lived in Miami my whole life and I have a love-hate relationship with it. I'm currently studying journalism at the University of Miami.

Another very important fact about me is I have three cats. I love them to death. I will never understand why cats get so much hate.

Aspirations

I graduate this semester, which means my career aspirations are starting to become too real. My main goal is to be able to write for an art or music magazine. I just love writing about art and artists. It gives me a lot of joy to write profiles about creatives or share cool things people are working on.

I also hope to eventually get out of Miami.

Music Taste

I've been a HUGE music fan my entire life. My mom started me off with artists like Morrissey, Peter Murphy, and The Cure. She was very displeased when it turned out I'm a sucker for pop. I tortured her with One Direction in middle school.

I spend a lot of time listening to music. In all honesty, I don't really stray away from my favorite artists too much. I think the familiarity is just really comforting.
